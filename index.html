Save consistently. Protect your future. Achieve your goals with certainty.

Flexible Long-Term Savings & Protection

A flexible long-term savings and protection solution designed to help you build wealth for education, family milestones, retirement, or major purchases—while ensuring your plans stay on course even if life takes an unexpected turn.

You save with discipline, enjoy guaranteed cash pay-outs, earn annual bonuses, and benefit from strong life protection throughout the policy term.

Key Benefits

  • Guaranteed maturity value up to 240% of Sum Assured
  • Flexible 1–9 guaranteed cash pay-outs
  • Annual compound bonuses declared yearly
  • Built-in life protection cover
  • 15% insurance tax relief (up to KES 60,000/year)

Life Protection Benefits

  • Natural death: 100% of Sum Assured + bonuses
  • Accidental death: 200% of Sum Assured + bonuses
  • Premium waiver on death claim
  • Savings continue to beneficiaries

Eligibility & Terms

  • Minimum entry age: 18 years
  • Maximum entry age: 55–60 years
  • Policy term: 5–20 years
  • Maturity age: Must mature before age 75
  • 6-month waiting period for natural death/illness
